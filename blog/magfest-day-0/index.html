MAGfest: Day 0

January 22, 2025 Bantz
Bantz Bytez

Started the morning with packin' up all that I needed/wanted. Had a hell of a time too. Stuffin' all I need in onto a carry-on wasn't easy, but I got it done. The ride to the airport was... somethin'. Almost missed my flight. Traffic's a motherfucker. Managed to make it minutes before the flight took off though.

Once I landed, I met with my friends: Naotox, Sylph, n' Foxcade. Meetin' Naotox was brief at first since we had to hightail it outta the airport pick up. Otherwise security would've hounded 'em to move their car. But, the ride we had gave us plenty of time to chat. Also got to meet one of their friends. Very sweet person. Thankful for Nao lettin' me crash at their hotel room for the entire time I'll be at MAGfest. :)

After the drive to the hotel, I got to meet one of my favorite white women, SYLPH!

She gifted me one of their pin sets she made! Dunno what it's referencin', but it's got all my favs in it! Kirby, PUSSY... cat, n' queers kissin'. Thank ya, Syl! I love these pins. Especially the eppy cat one. As thanks, I showed her my "controller polycule."

After settin' up my Soy Machine (Nintendo Switch) n' playing some Vesperia, Foxcade showed up! He wasn't sure I was me at first. He's never seen a Latino, since he's got this crippling disease called "Canadian" that prevents him from perceivin' melanin (no matter how slight). But, we hugged it out like brothers soon after I told him I was me. :)
