MAGfest 2025: The Final Days - Day 3 & 4

February 1, 2025 Bantz
Bantz Bytez

Felt like the week passed by in a day.

Spend the last days going to the arcades n' browsing the marketplace a little. Sylph n' I played a lot of stuff. In the Indie Arcade, we played this game called Gunfish. Party-like game. Various fish to choose that each have unique properties to their gun shot. Win condition is reducing your opponent's lives to zero. We played a bit of it n' Syl got a few points off me on me. BUT, being the GAYMER I am üò§ I won the match. Actually... idk I can't remember, but I'll just say I did :)

I later pulled Syl over to this game called Waddle Throttle. Caught my eye... cuz... penguins. I love penguins. The cabinet had an interesting control scheme. Only two action buttons per player. How ya control your penguin's speed is that mash the two buttons back and forth. Like imitating a waddle (GET IT?). Then to drift left or right, ya hold down a button. Hold down both buttons to slow your momentum.

Over all, Waddle Throttle's a fun game. Didn't get a change to play it, but the figure skating mode sounded like a great time. Syl n' I played the King of Hill mode. She won n' was the last penguin standin'! The "PENGWIN" win screen got a chuckle outta me üêß

We later went over to the arcade arcade. Forgot to take a pic, but it was funny seein' Daytona's arcade hardware out on a little stand they had. Dunno what it is, but I enjoy lookin' at printed circuit boards. Anyway, loved playin' this Daytona game. Always play on manual stick if I can since half of the fun is controlling that speed.

When we got the rhythm game section, Syl started playing this giant cabinet that had a piano-like controller. Interesting game, Nostalgia. Nothin' like playin' a real piano though. Mimics it just to enough to give the feelin' ya are. Unfortunately, it was hard to hear since MAGfest put all the rhythm games in a corner, so sounds overlapped. There was a headphone jack on the cabinet, but not everyone's carryin' 3.5mm headphones/IEMs (unlike me since I be like that). So, we had to get close to hear it.

Syl probably would do better if she could hear the music lol. Still pretty good tho.

Ended the 3rd day by playing around with the pinball games they had. Syl played this Godzilla themed one. Complained a lot that the game was a bit rigged. Ball kept being magnetized towards the gutter n' whatnot.

I played a bit of the Star Wars Mandalorian one that was right next to Syl. It was pretty much the same in terms of rigged difficulty. Syl got a pretty good score by the end of it. Despite the harsh difficulty lol.
